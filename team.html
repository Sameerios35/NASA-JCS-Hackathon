What if we had more time?

If our group had more time in order to complete our project, we would not only embrace the plan of development with our building data archives and population data, but we would take it one step further also include pollution and air quality data. This section of information would allow individuals, businesses, and the government decide what areas to develop.

Vision for the Future

Our goal is to give people a way to visualize the development of Houston and its infrastructure by providing data from the Harris County archives. These archives are confounded by the year and date each building was built. By analysing this information, it can assist the planning behind the growth of certain areas in Houston.
