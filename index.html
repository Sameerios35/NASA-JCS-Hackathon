Treasure Map for the Future

Houston Resources About Us Data Analysis

How does NASA benefit?

NASA has several ways to benefit from this project. With the data that our technology provides, it simplifies the process for NASA to locate and analyze buildings that may be in need of development. This can be used for NASA buildings and for properties that NASA wants to aquire in the future.

What if we had more time?

With given time in order to complete our project, we would not only embrace the plan of development with our building data archives and population data, but we would take it one step further also include pollution and air quality data. This section of information would allow individuals, businesses, and the government decide what areas to develop.

What is the vision for the future?

Our goal is to give people a way to visualize the development of Houston and its infrastructure by providing data from the Harris County archives. These archives are confounded by the year and date each building was built. By analysing this information, it can assist the planning towards the growth of certain areas in Houston.
